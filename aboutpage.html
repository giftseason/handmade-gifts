Welcome to the Gift Season!

The Gift Season is the hand-made concept store by Tatiana Bliznakova, the craftswoman with over 20 years of experience and distinguished by numerous awards.

Mission

I believe in the power of creativity and seek to inspire togetherness, kindness and friendship through creating meaningful and affordable jewellery, toys, and interior decor items. The prices of unique handmade gifts start just at 5$!

Beads, natural stones, leather, metal and wool are the primary materials I use for creating gifts for you.

So far, handmade gifts by the GiftSeason have brightened lives of people from Ukraine, Bulgaria, Germany, Australia, Canada and the USA.

Choose the best gift idea from a wide range of handmade Jewellery, Toys, Socks and Interior Decor Items. You can also order original gifts based on your own ideas or some of my previous works on Instagram. Gifts for any mood and any budget.

Values

I am happy that my creative ideas encourage people to express their feelings in sincere, heartfelt and unique ways.

This behaviour, in turn, creates more occasions for a happy life.

The most fascinating thing about my activity? The joy of giving happy moments whenever people ask me to create original presents for every occasion. This magical power of my handcrafted items - to create new gift seasons - has inspired the name of my shop Gift Season.

I strive to positively impact my customers' lives with Authenticity, Innovation, Passion, and Integrity.
